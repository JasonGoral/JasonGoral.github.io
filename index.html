Hello visitor and thank you for visiting Lukas' web page!

I created this website for a couple reasons, first to practice my coding and web design and second have an unique spot to archive my son Lukas' early years.

It will be nice to give him the link to this website when he is old enough to appreciate it and look back on a part of his life he may not remember that well.

As to the first point about learning to code and design, if you notice anything odd on your device or browser or just general suggestion you can let me know at JasonGorals@gmail.com

I hope you enjoy your browse and learning about all things Luka!
